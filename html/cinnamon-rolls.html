Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 - Tbsp honey
  • 1 - Envelope active dry yeast
  • 1-1/2 - Cups warm water
  • 3-3/4 - Cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 - Tsp salt
  • 2 - Tbsp olive oil
  • 6 - Tbsp butter at room temperature
  • 1 - Packed cup light brown sugar
  • 1 - Tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 - Cup powdered sugar
  • 1 - Tsp vanilla
  • 2 - Tbsp milk

The Dough

Combine honey, water and yeast in the bowl of a stand mixer until liquid becomes bubbly. Add flour, salt and oil. Knead in mixer for about 5 minutes or until dough forms a soft ball. Place in bowl, cover with plastic wrap and allow to rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.

Let's Bake

Preheat the oven to 375. Roll the dough on a lightly floured surface into a 13x9 inch rectangle. Mix butter, brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Spread butter mixture over dough with your fingers. Roll up the dough into a log. Cut crosswise into 12 equal slices and place on baking sheet 2 inches apart. Cover with plastic wrap and let rise for 30 minutes in a warm place. Bake for 18-20 minutes until golden. While rolls cool slightly, mix powdered sugar, milk and vanilla. Pour icing over still-warm cinnamon rolls. Serve immediately.
