Trailside Three Cheese Fondue

Quick and easy is the name of the game! Prep this dish at home and meal time is a snap.

At home combine:

  • 8oz Cream Cheese
  • 4oz Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 4oz Cojita Cheese
  • 1/4 Cup Heavy Whipping Cream

Let's Cook

Simmer over medium heat stirring regularly until everything is melted. Add more cream if necessary to reach the desired consistency.

Let everything cool.

Put the fondue in a zip-top and store it in the refrigerator until you are ready for the trail.

Don't freeze it! Cheese breaks down when frozen and you WILL NOT like the result!

If you pack it in a cooler with your other frozen goods, it will keep the length of your trip.

Serve It Up

When it's time to dig in just simmer the bag in some hot water for about 10 minutes.

Kneed the contents in the bag and return to the simmering water if necessary to soften to a dippable consistency.

Serve with your favorite veggies, crackers or chips.

Use the zip-top bag as a serving dish and cleanup becomes a cinch.
