Trailside Cheesecake

This delicious cheesecake was whipped up in a matter of minutes and devoured just as quick! With only 4 ingredients & a packweight of less that a pound it would be a crime to deny yourself something so delicious at the end of a long day. Or whenever the urge hits you!

Ingredients

  • 8oz - Cream Cheese
  • 2 - Tbsp Powdered Sugar
  • 1 - Tsp Vanilla
  • 4 - Large Cookies

At Home

Combine everything except the cookies in a mixing bowl and whip until completely incorporated.

Tranfer contents to a large zip-top bag and refridgerate.

On The Trail

Crumble cookies into pan or plate. Soft cookies work best. If you don't have these you will want to pack a Tbsp or two of oil to add to the cumbles to make them easier to form into the crust.

Form the cookie crumbs into the shape of the crust.

Remove the cream cheese mixture from the cooler and knead the contents until smooth.

Cut a bottom corner off the bag and pipe the mixture into the crust until full.

Using a spoon or spatula smooth the top.

Serve immediately or cover with foil and return to the cooler.


An alternative is to make these into individual desserts. This is a great way to get the kids involved in the mealtime festivities!

Have everyone crumble 1-2 cookies in a cup or small bowl. Pipe a small amount into each person's cup. Do it in layers for a yummy parfait style!
